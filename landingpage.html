About Selena Gomez

Selena Gomez is an American singer, actress, and producer. Born on July 22, 1992, in Grand Prairie, Texas, she first gained fame as a child actress, starring in the Disney Channel series "Wizards of Waverly Place."

Gomez's music career began in 2008, when she formed the pop-rock band Selena Gomez & the Scene. She later pursued a solo music career, releasing her debut album "Stars Dance" in 2013. Her subsequent albums, "Revival" (2015) and "Rare" (2020), solidified her position as a pop icon.

In addition to her music, Gomez has been involved in various philanthropic efforts, including advocating for mental health awareness and supporting organizations such as UNICEF and the Ryan Seacrest Foundation.
